The Bhim Festival is an annual music and arts festival curated by renowned Ghanaian artist Stonebowy.

It aims to celebrate Ghanaian culture, music, and creativity, bringing together local and international acts for a memorable experience. This year marks a decade of the festival’s dominance in the music scene, promising an even grander celebration.
